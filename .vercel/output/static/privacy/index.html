Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Effective Date: January 2025

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

We collect information you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Contact information — If you email us, we receive your email address and message content
  • Newsletter subscription — If you subscribe, we collect your email address

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our site, we automatically collect:

  • Usage data — Pages visited, time spent, referring sites, and general navigation patterns
  • Device information — Browser type, operating system, and device type
  • IP address — Used for general location (country/region) and security purposes

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Improve our content and user experience
  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Send newsletters (if subscribed)
  • Analyze site traffic and usage patterns
  • Prevent fraud and maintain security

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Cookies and Tracking

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help websites function and gather usage information.

Cookies We Use

  • Essential cookies — Required for basic site functionality
  • Analytics cookies — Help us understand how visitors use the site (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Affiliate cookies — Track affiliate link clicks for commission purposes

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect site functionality.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information:

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand site usage. Google Analytics collects anonymous data about your visit. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Affiliate Networks

When you click affiliate links, our affiliate partners (including Amazon) may place cookies on your device. These cookies track your purchase for commission purposes. See our Affiliate Disclosure for more information.

Hosting

Our site is hosted by third-party providers who may collect standard server logs including IP addresses, access times, and pages requested.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no internet transmission is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmitted to our site.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access — Request a copy of information we hold about you
  • Correction — Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your information
  • Opt-out — Unsubscribe from marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us.

Children's Privacy

Our site is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us.

External Links

Our site contains links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of other sites. We encourage you to review privacy policies of sites you visit.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
