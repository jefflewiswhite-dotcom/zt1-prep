## Hub-and-Spoke Model

### Hub 1: Everyday Readiness (Start Here)
**URL:** `/start-here`
**Purpose:** Entry point for complete beginners. Explains the "zero to one" philosophy.

Spokes:
- What is everyday readiness?
- First steps: 5 things to do this week
- Common emergencies to prepare for
- Links to all other hubs

### Hub 2: Bug Out Bag (BOB)
**URL:** `/guides/bug-out-bag`
**Purpose:** Complete guide to building a 72-hour evacuation bag.

Spokes (Item Clusters):
- **Shelter:** Emergency bivvies, sleeping bags, tarps
- **Water:** Filters, purification, bottles, containers
- **Fire:** Lighters, ferro rods, tinder, stoves
- **Light:** Flashlights, headlamps, lanterns
- **First Aid:** Kits, IFAKs, medications
- **Tools:** Knives, multitools, saws
- **Food:** Ration bars, freeze-dried meals
- **Comms/Power:** Radios, batteries, power banks
- **Navigation:** Maps, compasses, GPS
- **Documents:** Copies, waterproof storage

### Hub 3: Get Home Bag (GHB)
**URL:** `/guides/get-home-bag`
**Purpose:** Compact bag to get home from work/commute during emergencies.

Spokes:
- Same categories as BOB but focused on compact/lightweight options
- Urban vs. suburban vs. rural variations
- Vehicle-based vs. office-based storage

### Hub 4: 72-Hour Kit for Families
**URL:** `/guides/72-hour-kit`
**Purpose:** Home-based emergency supplies for sheltering in place.

Spokes:
- Per-person supplies
- Family-specific needs (kids, elderly, pets)
- Storage solutions
- Rotation schedules

### Hub 5: Car Emergency Kit
**URL:** `/guides/car-emergency-kit`
**Purpose:** Vehicle-specific emergency supplies.

Spokes:
- Breakdown essentials (jumper cables, tire repair)
- Weather emergencies (winter, summer, floods)
- First aid for vehicles
- Signaling and safety

### Hub 6: Power Outage Preparedness
**URL:** `/guides/power-outage-preparedness`
**Purpose:** Home preparation for power loss.

Spokes:
- Lighting solutions
- Power banks and solar chargers
- Portable power stations
- Food preservation
- Heating/cooling alternatives
- Communication during outages

### Hub 7: Water Storage & Purification
**URL:** `/guides/water-storage-purification`
**Purpose:** Everything about emergency water.

Spokes:
- Storage containers
- Water filters (gravity, pump, straw)
- Purification tablets
- Rain collection basics
- Finding water sources

### Hub 8: First Aid Kit Basics
**URL:** `/guides/first-aid-kit-basics`
**Purpose:** General preparedness first aid (non-medical advice).

Spokes:
- Pre-made kits vs. building your own
- Essential supplies checklist
- Specialized kits (trauma, burns, bites)
- Training resources (link to Red Cross, etc.)

## Internal Linking Strategy

### From Hubs to Spokes
Each hub/pillar article links to:
- All relevant spoke articles in that category
- Related hubs (e.g., BOB links to GHB, 72-hour kit)
- Relevant gear roundups

### From Spokes to Hubs
Each spoke article links to:
- Parent hub at beginning and end
- 1-2 related spoke articles
- Relevant comparison articles

### Cross-Hub Linking
- Water articles link to BOB, GHB, and 72-hour kit
- Lighting articles link to BOB, GHB, car kit, and power outage
- First aid links to all bag types
